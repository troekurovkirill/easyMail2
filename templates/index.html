MacDroid

An app that easily
connects Android to Mac

MacDroid is one of the few apps that can easily transfer files between Android and Mac, letting you access and manage files in the Finder without the necessity of having any third-party apps on your Android device.
Requires macOS 10.10 and higher;
Compatible with all Android and MTP devices.

Mount Android
as disk on Mac

With MacDroid you can easily connect any Android or MTP device to your Mac and manage its content directly in the Finder. MacDroid gives you access to both — internal and external — storages of Android.

Transfer any type of files between Mac and Android

MacDroid can transfer any type of files between your Mac and Android — pictures, videos, music, separate files or even entire folders.
